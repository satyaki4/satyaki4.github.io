Alhambra, Granada

Welcome! I am a postdoc at the Department of Economic Policy, Università Cattolica del Sacro Cuore, Milano.

My research is in the economics of innovation and intellectual property. I study how patent institutions shape incentives for R&D. I have three lines of research: pharmaceutical patents extensions and its role in fostering drug innovation the EU, the geography of innovation in India, and the effects of U.S. patent reform, the America Invents Act on small inventors.
