Welcome! I am a postdoc at the Department of Economic Policy, Università Cattolica del Sacro Cuore, Milano.

My research is in the economics of innovation and intellectual property. I study how patent institutions shape incentives for R&D — with a focus on pharmaceutical patents and supplementary protection certificates in Europe, the geography of innovation in India, and the effects of U.S. patent reform on small inventors.
